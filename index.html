Imani

Imani is a locally owned, locally staffed company—not a franchise that is based in Seattle, Washington.
We provide services to assist seniors at all levels of need from running errands to end-of-life care.


Our compassionate, professional caregivers enable seniors to live
more comfortably and independently in the familiar surroundings of their own home.
We also frequently work with clients in senior apartments, assisted living facilities, dementia care units,
nursing homes, hospital, hotels and other settings.


We serve seniors throughout the greater King County area,
whether they’re living in their own home, in an assisted living or skilled nursing facility, or with family members.
We also provide caregivers during hospital stays to make sure your loved one is not alone
or lacking for the attention and information they need.

Caregiver Education

We believe that only the best education will facilitate the best client care.
As a result, Imani Care has one of the most thorough educational programs in the Seattle area.

Each caregiver is required to attend a full-day orientation, which includes information about company policies and procedures,
a review of skills with a registered nurse, and even a cooking class! Our office team attends an orientation as well.

Caregivers are also required by law to complete 12 hours of continuing education credit each year.
A Helping Hand takes this responsibility very seriously. We therefore provide state-approved lessons and quizzes on a range
of topics critical to providing care to our clients.
